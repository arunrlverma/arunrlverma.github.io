Questions

Before you upload.

Straight answers. If something isn't here, email me directly.

Is this medical advice?

No — it's an educational and research tool, not a medical device, diagnosis, or treatment plan. Review any finding with your oncology team before acting.

What files do I need?

Existing sequencing from your tumor (ideally with a matched normal), plus any lab or pathology reports. Command Center analyzes files — it doesn't generate them.

How long does it take?

The pipeline runs automatically once your files are uploaded. You'll be notified when the report is ready.

What if it doesn't find much?

Some cases carry fewer signals. The report still organizes what's there and flags gaps — no finding is guaranteed, and that honesty is the point.

Can my doctor use it?

That's the goal — organized questions with source notes, ready for an oncologist or second opinion. It supports their judgment, not replaces it.

What about refunds?

Analysis runs as soon as you buy, so fees are generally non-refundable. Review the example report first.
